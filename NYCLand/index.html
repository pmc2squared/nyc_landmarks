The City of New York is the most populous city in the United States. It has been the country's largest city since 1790. New York served as the capital of the United States from 1785 until 1790. It has been described uniquely as the cultural, financial, and media capital of the world. Many districts and landmarks in New York City are well known, and the city received a record 62.8 million tourists in 2017, hosting three of the world's ten most visited tourist attractions in 2013. This site is dedicated to exploring some of the most iconic structures in New York City.
